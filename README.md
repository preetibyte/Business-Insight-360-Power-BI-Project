# Overview  

The Business 360 Dashboard is an end-to-end data analytics solution built using Power BI to provide a comprehensive view of business performance across multiple departments such as Finance, Sales, Marketing, and Supply Chain.
This project transforms raw data into actionable insights, enabling stakeholders to make informed decisions.  

## Objectives  
- Provide a 360-degree view of business operations.
- Track KPIs and performance metrics across departments.
- Enable data-driven decision-making.
- Improve visibility into revenue, costs, and profitability.

## Tools & Technologies  
Power BI – Data visualization & dashboard creation  
SQL – Data extraction and transformation  
Excel/CSV – Data sources  
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Measures & calculations  
Power Query – Data cleaning and shaping  

## Dashboard Features

### Executive Overview  
- High-level KPIs (Revenue, Profit, Growth %)
- Year-over-Year comparison
- Trend analysis

### Finance View  
- Profit & Loss statement
- Cost breakdown
- Margin analysis

### Sales View  
- Sales performance by region/product
- Top & bottom customers
- Sales trends

### Marketing View  
- Campaign performance
- Customer segmentation
- Conversion rates

### Supply Chain View  
- Inventory levels
- Order fulfillment
- Delivery performance

## Key Insights  
- Revenue Growth Trends: The business showed consistent growth over time, with certain periods contributing significantly higher revenue due to seasonal demand and targeted strategies.
- Top Performing Segments: A small group of products/customers contributed to a large share of revenue, highlighting the importance of focusing on high-value segments.
- Profitability Analysis: While revenue increased, some regions/products had lower profit margins due to higher operational or discounting costs.
- Customer Behavior: Repeat customers generated a substantial portion of sales, indicating strong customer retention but also revealing opportunities to expand the new customer base.
- Regional Performance Variations: Certain regions outperformed others in both sales and profitability, suggesting differences in market demand and operational efficiency.
- Cost Optimization Opportunities: Analysis identified areas with high costs and low returns, enabling better allocation of resources and cost control strategies.
- Supply Chain Efficiency: Delays in delivery and inventory imbalances affected overall performance, emphasizing the need for improved demand forecasting and logistics planning.
